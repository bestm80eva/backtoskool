F7D7: Place a continual subcommand routine address into a character's buffer
Used by command lists 32, 34, 36, 38, 40, 42, 44, 46, 48, 50, 52, 54, 56 and 76. Collects a continual subcommand routine address from the command list and places it into bytes 23 and 24 of the character's buffer. The routine address will be one of the following:
Address Description
775B Make BOY WANDER fire his catapult now and then
F4CC Make ALBERT keep an eye out for ERIC during lessons
F7DF Make ANGELFACE stalk HAYLEY
F804 Make ANGELFACE or BOY WANDER hit or fire now and then
Input
H Character number (205-207)
Output
F7D7 LD L,$17 Collect the routine address from the command list and place it into bytes 23 and 24 of the character's buffer
F7D9 CALL $6264
F7DC JP $6381 Move to the next command in the command list
