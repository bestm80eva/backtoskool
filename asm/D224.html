D224: Make a character do nothing
Used by the routines at 5F1C and F5BE, and by command lists 0, 2, 4, 6, 8, 32, 34, 46, 48, 50 and 52 to make a character do nothing. The RET instruction is located in ERIC's buffer at byte 36, which is otherwise unused.
Input
Output
D224 RET Return having done nothing
