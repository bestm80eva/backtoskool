26263: Deal with a character who is looking for a seat
The address of this uninterruptible subcommand routine is placed into bytes 17 and 18 of a character's buffer by the routine at 26239 (after he's been knocked out of his chair and just got up to look for another one - or the same one, in EINSTEIN's case), or by the routine at 26304.
