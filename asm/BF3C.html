BF3C: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 191
Used by the routine at 6464. Skool region 191 corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see the skool region data table at B93C) or in mid-air (see B73C and B83C).
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 191 to the destination region (189-196). Region 192 is the assembly hall stage (see 6DA3).
